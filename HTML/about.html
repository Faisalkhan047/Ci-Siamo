CI SIAMO

Ci Siamo translates to "here we are" or "we've finally arrived." It's the clinking of

glasses after a long week, the rush of arriving at a new destination, the relief of a

familiar smile — it's an exhalation.

Celebrating the beauty of simplicity, Chef Hillary Sterling centers her Italian-

inflected menu around live-fire cooking and seasonal ingredients, bridging the

        traditional with the contemporary in a transportive yet familiar space.
